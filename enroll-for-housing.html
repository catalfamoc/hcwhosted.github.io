Share your quarantine experience

Many healthcare workers are trying to decide whether to self-quarantine.

By participating voluntarily in a research study of frontline health care workers, you will provide data on the impact of self-quarantine, to improve pandemic preparation and to optimize outcomes for patients, clinicians, and the community.

The Voluntary Self-Quarantine (VOSQUA) research study is NOT required to participate in the HCW HOSTED cohort or to receive housing and resources.

If you are interested in participating in a short baseline survey, click the link below.

Reduced Rate Housing

The goals of HCW HOSTED are to provide free housing during the COVID-19 response, to support your mental health, and ensure your family's overall well being. That said, the free housing HCW HOSTED finds may not be ideal to your specific needs.

There are other options out there. Private rental organizations and hotels have already begun to support responders. Below are listed reduced rate ($) hotel and housing resources you might also consider. HCW HOSTED is here to help you find any housing and will provide you and your family all the same support during the COVID-19 response effort.

Airbnb

Airbnb is offering no-fees on housing for COVID-19 first responders, find available rental properties here.

If you want to offer your property via Airbnb, you can Offer your space or donate ($) here.

Hotels

VISIT Tucson has identified over forty local hotels offering reduced rates and services to COVID-19 responders.

Many hospital human resources departments are working directly with large hotel groups to obtain free or reduced nightly rates. Contact your HR department to see what resources are available.

HCW HOSTED can help get you into a hotel of your preference, after we have your enrollment information.
