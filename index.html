HCW HOSTED is a Tucson-based initiative created to coordinate services such as emotional health support and symptom monitoring administered by service providers and researchers from within the Tucson community and University of Arizona.

Our goal is to assure the wellbeing of healthcare workers throughout the course of the COVID-19 pandemic.

HCW HOSTED provides support to healthcare workers (HCWs) who are looking for housing during the expected COVID-19 surge to prevent infection of family members by helping them find a comfortable place to stay.

This is a community-led grassroots initiative with a goal of addressing the immediate need to slow the spread of COVID-19, while also thinking long-term to protect the mental health of our healthcare workforce, which is already under-staffed and over-burdened.

This program offers more than just a room to healthcare workers at the frontline of COVID-19.
