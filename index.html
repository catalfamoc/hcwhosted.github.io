HCW HOSTED is a Tucson, Arizona, community-based initiative created to assure the wellbeing of healthcare workers (HCWs) throughout the course of the COVID-19 pandemic. The initiative also supports the families of HCWs. Our goal is to prevent family members from becoming infected by helping HCWs find suitable housing to self-quarantine.

In addition to housing, we will monitor the ongoing health status of HCWs, connect them to mental health services as needed, and offer social support services for their partners and children.

HCW HOSTED offers more than just a room to those at the frontline of COVID-19.
