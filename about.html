HCW-HOSTED

This is the page meant to provide you with additional information about the HCW-HOSTED initiative in several key areas.

Mission Statement

Website mission

  1. Enroll interested Health Care Workers (HCW) in HOSTED
  2. Provide information on this community-driven effort

Initiative's effort

  • To offer physical and psychosocial support to healthcare workers (HCWs) and protection for their families and communities by providing dedicated HCW housing for HCWs in cities implementing HOSTED throughout the COVID-19 pandemic surge.
  • Main service
    • Quarantined housing (separate areas for 1) asymptomatic and working 2) symptomatic with testing pending, 3) +COVID-19 not working, and 4) Post-tour of duty quarantine awaiting re-entry into home
  • Secondary service
    • Instrumental support (food, laundry, etc) Social support, pysch support, monitoring (epi surveillance)

Why is HOSTED housing important?

  • The ability to protect physicians and their families is a big factor in the resilience of the healthcare system. HOSTED can reduce transmission in the Healthcare Workforce (HCW) families.
  • Psychosocial support in HOSTED may also reduce trauma and PTSD in the HCW and among their families.
  • The way the COVID-19 outbreak was ended in China was by isolating healthcare workers.
